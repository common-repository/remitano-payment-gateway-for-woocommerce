Enable Remitano Payment Gateway on your WooCommerce system to allow customers to pay with cryptocurrency.

== Description ==

The Remitano Payment Gateway is a simple tool that allows users to quickly pay for goods and services using cryptocurrencies such as USDT, Bitcoin, and Ethereum. This payment method is adaptable to businesses of all sizes, particularly online stores. Besides, merchants have the opportunity to **expand their business to over 3 Million Remitano global cryptocurrency investors.**

**Remitano payment service:**

= Payment options =

Customers are flexible to choose one of the three available options:
* Use Remitano coin wallet
* Buy cryptocurrency directly within 5 minutes with fiat currencies such as: NGN, SGD, VND...
* Deposit coins into the wallet (zero confirmation)

= Supported coins =

Remitano currently supports payments in **6 cryptocurrencies**:

* Bitcoin (BTC)
* Ethereum (ETH)
* Tether (USDT)
* Ripple (XRP)
* Litecoin (LTC)
* Bitcoin Cash (BCH)

= Service charge =

* No transaction fee
* No registration fee
* No maintenance fee

Find out more about Remitano payment gateway [here](https://remitano.com/btc/payment_gateway).

== Installation ==

_* You can check the full version of installation guide including images on [our website](https://developers.remitano.com/docs/payment-gateway/plugins/woocommerce)_

To accept crypto payment on your WooCommerce store with Remitano Payment Gateway, you just simply follow these steps:

### 1. Download and install Remitano Payment Gateway plugin for WooCommerce
![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18634/unnamed.png)

**Step 1:** To download and install the Remitano Payment Gateway plugin, from your WordPress admin page, go to **'Plugins > Add New'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18635/unnamed__1_.png)

**Step 2:** Then search for **'Remitano Payment Gateway for WooCommerce'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18637/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.09.47_PM.png)

**Step 3:** Click on the **'Install Now'** button to install the plugin. Once it has been installed, click **'Activate'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18645/unnamed__4_.png)

### 2. Get the API Key and Secret
You need to login into your [Remitano Account](http://Remitano.com) and follow this [instruction](https://developers.remitano.com/docs/guides/generate-new-key) to get the access keys:

**Step 1:** Click on the **'Settings'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18638/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.22.34_PM.png)

**Step 2:** Scroll to the **'API access'** section at Settings page and click on **'Generate new key'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18639/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.22.55_PM.png)

**Step 3:** After the process is completed, you will get your API keys including: **KEY** and **SECRET**. Copy these information then you can use them later to link your Remitano account with your WooCommerce Store.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18640/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.23.07_PM.png)

### 3. Link Remitano Payment Gateway with your WooCommerce store
**Step 1:** Within the WordPress administration page, go to the **'WooCommerce > Settings'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18641/unnamed__3_.png)

**Step 2:** Click on the **'Payments'** Tab.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18642/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.10.39_PM.png)

**Step 3:** Turn on the Enable button.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18643/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.51.21_PM.png)

**Step 4:** Click on **'Set up'**.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18644/Screen_Shot_2021-08-05_at_3.51.29_PM.png)

**Step 5:** Copy access key and fill in **'Live API key'**  and copy secret key and fill in **'Secret API key'**. Click **'Save changes'** and the gateway will be active.

![](https://img-portal-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/image/attachment/18555/pasted_image_0.png)

That's all! Your customers are now able to checkout and pay with cryptocurrency such as USDT, Bitcoin, Etherium.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I test an integration? =

Yes we have a sandbox account where you can test and see a demonstration of the flow. Please use this [link](https://developers.remitano.com/docs/payment-gateway/quick-start/) to access our sandbox.

= How can I integrate Remitano Payment Gateway to my website? =

You can easily follow the instructions [here](https://developers.remitano.com/docs/payment-gateway/plugins/woocommerce) to integrate.

= How can my customer pay for your service? =

They are flexible to choose one of the three available options: Use Remitano coin wallet, buy cryptocurrency from Remitano P2P or deposit coins into the wallet.

= How can I contact Remitano for help? =

You can contact our support team via Remitano.com website chatbox or by sending an email to [bizdev@remitano.com](mailto:bizdev@remitano.com) When contacting Remitano support, please describe your issue and attach screenshots.
